About Us

The Open Ocean Collective is dedicated to creating a more diverse and inclusive surfing community in Britain. We work to break down barriers and build pathways for people from all backgrounds to enjoy and participate in surfing.

Through community events, mentorship programs, and advocacy, we're working to transform British surfing into a space where everyone feels welcome and valued.

Whether you're a beginner taking your first paddle or an experienced surfer looking to give back, there's a place for you in the Open Ocean Collective.
